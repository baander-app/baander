Bånder Desktop is a fast, privacy-respecting music player for your self-hosted Bånder server.

• Stream and browse your personal library with smooth search and playlists.
• Built for performance and reliability with modern Electron and web tech.
• You control your data — Bånder Desktop connects to your own server.
